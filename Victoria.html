The Landscapes of Victoria

Grampians National Park

Image Source

This park is located in the Grampians region of Victoria.It is one of the richest Aboriginal rock art sites in south-eastern Australia.The Grampians feature a series of mountain ranges of sandstone and rock art.The Grampians had multiple indigenous names,one of which was Gariwerd in the western Australian Aboriginal languages of Mukjarawaint, Jardwadjali and Djab.

Great Otway National Park

Image Source

The Great Otway Nationa Park is situated within the Otway ranges and contains diverse range of landscapes and vegetation.Historically several wildfires have burnt through the park in this century which shape its ecology and plant and animal diversity.The last major fire was in 1983.The park is famous for interstate and international tourists with sevral companies operating tours within the region.
