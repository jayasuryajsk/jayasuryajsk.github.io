Australia

Australia is a continent and an island located between Indian Ocean and South Pacific Ocean.Properly called as Commonwealth of Australia,its territory consists of entire continent and smaller outlying islands.This makes it the 6th larfers country in world by area.Australia's capital is Canberra and it's largest city is sydney.The country's major metro areas are Melbourne,Brisbane,Perth and Adelaide.

History

Before the arrival of Europeans,Indigenous Australians inhabited the continent for about 65 thousand years.The people migrated by land bridges and short sea crossings from what is now Southeast Asia.Aboriginal Australian culture is one of the oldest continual cultures on Earth.
